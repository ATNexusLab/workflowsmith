# Final Answer Checklist

Before giving a final answer, confirm:

- The user's request was answered.
- Changed files are summarized when repository files changed.
- Verification commands are listed.
- Blockers or skipped checks are stated.
- Next import or review step is clear when relevant.

Keep the final answer concise and tied to completed work.
